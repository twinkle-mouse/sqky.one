### Object Possession

So, this ritual is very useful and you’ll find it very often with many many applications.
Essentially, if you have something with a significant relation to something(someone) else, you can pass on that relation to another object.

This “something with a significant relation” could be anything: a piece of clothing, a plushie, a bowl, a comb, a pen; or even a part of someone’s body: their hair, their tears, their blood, their fingernails<sup>(ew)</sup>, their skin<sup>(ewwww)</sup>…you get the idea.

You don’t need a wand or any trinkets for this ritual, but you do need to *draw* it.
The ritual is as follows:

1. Gather the object you want to “possess” or “infuse”—the target—and the object you want to pass on the relation of—the source—and draw a circle on the ground with a **white chalk**.
2. Draw two inner circles that sit opposite of each other, on the far ends of the outer circle (but not touching it!).
   Place the target on one and the source on the other.
3. Draw two **equilateral triangles** the vertices of which *touch* the outer circle, the *origin* of the outer circle, and the inner circle.
4. Kneel down on the *origin* of the ritual (and make sure you can reach the two objects), close your eyes, and chant:
   “<mark>With the ritual I have prepared, you two shall be linked for (insert period of time, <i>please</i> don’t say eternity)</mark>”
5. (With eyes still closed) Grab the two objects and make the two touch, and now they should be linked for your specified period of time!

Something to know is that: all foci are really just *crystals* imbued with magic, usually made into an orb because orbs are slick and look nice and have a nice touch, but they don’t *necessarily* need to be perfect spheres.

You can make a Focus of Ghostly Reach with the following recipe:

1. Get an amethyst gem. Again, the convention is that it’d be a sphere, but like, it doesn’t *need* to be.
2. Measure the diameter of the tip of your wand. It’s typically 2.5cm.
3. Drill a hole in the gem that matches the tip, and confirm that you can stick the focus onto the wand.
4. Get some oil. You can use any oil you have on hand for this, it just needs to be oily (?).
5. Get some air powder and mix it with the oil, then cover your gem with the air-y paste.
6. Put it in a small pot filled with 1L of water and very slowly heat it for 6 hours, with a lid on.
   Make sure the heat is low enough that you don’t just boil all the water off.
7. Afterwards, remove all the water, sit the gem in a dry area and let it cool off, and wipe it with some cloth.
